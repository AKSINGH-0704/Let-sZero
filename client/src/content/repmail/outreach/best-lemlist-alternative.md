Lemlist is a polished multichannel outreach suite, and its visual personalization is genuinely distinctive. Teams who look for an alternative usually do so for one of two reasons: per-seat pricing that scales with headcount, or the realization that for cold email specifically, heavy visual and HTML personalization can work against deliverability. The alternatives differ by pricing model and by how they approach personalization. Pricing below was verified in July 2026; confirm current numbers before buying.

## What to look for in an alternative

Consider two things Lemlist makes you weigh. **Cost structure**: per-seat pricing ($79 to $109 per user per month) means a five-person team pays five times the rate before sending anything, so if your variable is volume rather than headcount, consumption pricing fits better. **Personalization style**: Lemlist leans on visual, image-based personalization, which is engaging but raises the HTML-to-text ratio that pushes cold mail toward Promotions. A text-first alternative optimizes for the inbox instead.

## The main alternatives

**RepMail** is the text-first, metered alternative. It prices by credit rather than by seat, sends through AWS SES, and personalizes with GPT-4o at the level of wording and sentence structure rather than images, which keeps messages plain enough to read as Primary-tab mail while still being genuinely per-recipient. Real-time AWS SNS suppression protects the domain.

**Smartlead** is the alternative for high-volume, unlimited-mailbox sending at $39 to $379 a month.

**Instantly** is the alternative if you want a flat plan with a built-in leads database, at $47 to $97 a month.

If deliverability is the trigger, [why Lemlist mail lands in spam](/repmail/learn/outreach/lemlist-emails-going-to-spam) is worth reading before you move, because the common causes are configuration and personalization choices that follow you to any other tool. ## The honest verdict

If you need Lemlist's multichannel breadth and visual personalization, an alternative will feel narrower, that is genuinely Lemlist's strength. But if you are leaving over per-seat cost or because visual personalization is hurting your cold deliverability, RepMail's metered, text-first, SES-based model is the most direct answer. As always, the tool sets the ceiling; your list and authentication set the outcome.
